# Fautuaga a Le Faatonu

E tofu tagata uma ma a latou lava faatonu e sili ona fiafia i ai `Markdown`

Matou [Markdown Preview Enhanced](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=shd101wyy.markdown-preview-enhanced) fa'aoga [vscode](https://code.visualstudio.com/) Tusia `MarkDown`

E pei ona faʻaalia i lalo, kiliki-saʻo i le faatonu e tatala ai le faʻamalama faʻaaliga.

<img alt="" src="https://p.3ti.site/1720775043.avif">

`code xxx.md` i luga o le laina faʻatonu e valaʻau `vscode` tatala `Markdown` faila.

Fa'aoga [PicList](https://github.com/Kuingsmile/PicList) e fa'apipi'i ata, manatua e seti le ki fa'apipi'i, ma fa'apipi'i le fa'amalama i le kiliki e tasi.

E lagolagoina otometi le kopiina o le igoa faila `Markdown`

I le taimi lava e tasi, e mafai ona e vaʻai i le faʻasologa o loʻo i lalo, suia le faila, ma faʻapipiʻi le faila faila e otometi ona faʻapipiʻi i le `avif`
