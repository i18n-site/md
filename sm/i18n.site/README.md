# i18n.site: Fa'asologa O Le Upega Tafa'ilagi E Tele Gagana

`i18n.site` Tele-gagana, fa'ato'a tu'u fa'atupu fa'amaumauga.

O lo'o avanoa nei i le taimi muamua, ae o lo'o iai pea vaega taua o lo'o i lalo ole atina'e Fa'amolemole va'ai [le fuafuaga o le atina'e](/i18n.site/c/roadmap) mo fa'amatalaga.

Fa'amolemole <button onclick="mailsub()">kiliki lenei imeli e lesitala i fa'afouga oloa</button> ma <button onclick="webpush()">fa'aagaioi le browser push</button> .

Fa'afeiloa'i fo'i e mulimuli / matou tala [i18n-site.bsky.social](https://bsky.app/profile/i18n-site.bsky.social) [X.COM: @i18nSite](https://x.com/i18nSite)

## Upu Tomua

`i18n.site` o lo'u toe mafaufau i le fa'ata'ita'iga o le atina'eina o le upega tafa'ilagi.

Ole mea lea ole fa'apipi'i fa'amaumauga ole upegatafa'ilagi ma le auivi o le atina'eina ole upega tafa'ilagi.

O se faʻataʻitaʻiga fou o le atinaʻeina o le upega tafaʻilagi e faʻaogatotonu `MarkDown`

O vaega taʻitasi pito i luma o se afifi e mafai ona faʻapipiʻi tutoʻatasi.

I luga o le faʻavae o le vavaeeseina o le pito i luma ma le pito i tua, o loʻo i ai foi le vavaeeseina o mea faʻamautu ma faʻamatalaga faʻamalosi.

O le mea o loʻo e asia [i18n.site](/) e fausia e faʻavae i luga o lenei faʻavae (e aofia ai le faiga faʻaoga, faiga faʻapipiʻi, imeli imeli, ma isi).
