# Nuŋlɔla Ƒe Kafukafu

Amesiame kple eƒe nuŋlɔla si wòlɔ̃na wu Mina míagblɔ míaƒe nuwɔna nyuitɔwo le afisia `Markdown`

Míezãa [vscode](https://code.visualstudio.com/) [Markdown Preview Enhanced](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=shd101wyy.markdown-preview-enhanced) `MarkDown`

Abe alesi woɖee fia le ete ene la, zi ɖusi dzi le nuŋlɔla la me be nàʋu ŋgɔdonya ƒe fesrea.
