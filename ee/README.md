## Ŋgɔdonya

Internet ɖe didiƒe si le yamenutome ŋutɔŋutɔwo me ɖa, gake gbegbɔgblɔ ƒe vovototowo gakpɔtɔ le mɔ xem na amegbetɔwo ƒe ɖekawɔwɔ.

Togbɔ be gbegɔmeɖeɖe le web-browser la me hã la, mɔ̃ siwo dia nyatakakawo mete ŋu biaa nya le gbegbɔgblɔ vovovowo me kokoko o.

To hadomenyatakakadzraɖoƒewo kple e-mail dzi la, ezu numame na amewo be woƒe susu nanɔ nyatakakatsoƒewo ŋu le woawo ŋutɔwo degbe me.

Esi nyatakakawo le wowóm eye xexeame katã ƒe asitsatsa le edzi yim ta la, be woate ŋu aʋli ho le amewo ƒe susu si mebɔ o ta la, **gbegbɔgblɔ geɖe dodo ɖe ŋgɔ nye aɖaŋu vevi aɖe** .

Ne enye ame ŋutɔ ƒe dɔ si wowɔ le mɔ gbadza nu si di be yeakpɔ ŋusẽ ɖe nyasela geɖe wu dzi hã la, ele be wòawɔ dukɔwo dome mɔ̃ɖaŋununya tiatia tso gɔmedzedzea me ke.

Sedede ƒe dɔwɔnu ( [source code](https://github.com/i18n-site/rust/tree/main/i18) ) si ɖea `Markdown` kple `YAML` gɔme ɖe gbegbɔgblɔ geɖe me.

Ate ŋu alé `Markdown` ƒe nɔnɔme me ɖe asi bliboe. Ate ŋu ade dzesi faɛl ƒe tɔtrɔwo eye wòaɖe faɛl siwo wotrɔ ko gɔme.

Woate ŋu atrɔ asi le gɔmeɖeɖea ŋu.

Trɔ asi le nuŋɔŋlɔ gbãtɔa ŋu eye nàgaɖe egɔme kple mɔ̃ ake, womaŋlɔ asitɔtrɔ siwo wowɔ kple asi le gɔmeɖeɖea ŋu o (ne wometrɔ asi le nuŋɔŋlɔ gbãtɔa ƒe memama sia ŋu o).

Àte ŋu azã dɔwɔnu siwo nènya wu atsɔ atrɔ asi le `Markdown` ŋu (gake màte ŋu atsɔ memamawo akpe ɖe eŋu alo atutu wo o), eye nàzã mɔ si nènya nyuie wu atsɔ awɔ gɔmeɖeɖea dzi kpɔkpɔ.
