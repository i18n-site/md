# Bothata Bjo Bo Tlwaelegilego

## Go Oketša Goba Go Phumola Methalo Ya Phetolelo, Go Feleletša Ka Kgakanego Phetolelong

Gopola **gore palo ya methalo yeo e lego phetolelong e swanetše go sepelelana le methalo yeo e lego mongwalong wa mathomo** .

Ke gore, ge o beakanya phetolelo ka seatla, **o se ke wa oketša goba wa phumola mela ya phetolelo** , go sego bjalo kamano ya go dira mmapa magareng ga phetolelo le mongwalo wa mathomo e tla ba yeo e sa rulaganywago.

Ge e ba o oketša goba o phumola mola ka phošo, o baka kgakanego, hle bušetša phetolelo phetolelong pele ga phetošo, sepetša phetolelo `i18` , gomme o boloke gape mmapa o nepagetšego.

Go beakanya magareng ga phetolelo le sengwalwa sa mathomo `.i18h/hash` tlemilwe go leswao la go hlama leswao le lefsa ka go [i18n.site/token](//i18n.site/token)

## `YAML` : Ya Go Thibela `HTML` Ya Kgokagano Go Fetošetšwa Go `Markdown`

Boleng bja `YAML` bo tla swarwa bjalo ka `MarkDown` bakeng sa phetolelo.

Ka nako ye nngwe phetošo ya `HTML` → `MarkDown` ga se seo re se nyakago, go swana le `<a href="/">Home</a>` go fetošetšwa go `[Home](/)` .

Oketša seka sefe goba sefe ntle le `href` go leswao la `a` , go swana le `<a class="A" href="/">Home</a>` , go efoga phetošo ye.

Phimola difaele tše di thulanago gomme o sepele gape `i18`
