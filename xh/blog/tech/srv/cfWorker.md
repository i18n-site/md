
## CNAME Ibotshelelwe Igama Lesizinda

Igama lesizinda libotshelelwe nge `SSL/TLS` → igama lomamkeli wesiko CNAME Cloudflare Worker lesizinda libotshiwe, ufikelelo oluthe ngqo luza kuxela impazamo `error code: 522`

Ngeli xesha, kufuneka usebenzise indlela yeHTTP → indlela yabasebenzi ukubopha indlela, njengoko kubonisiwe ngezantsi.
