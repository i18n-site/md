## <a rel=id href="#ha" id="ha"></a> Ukufumaneka okuphezulu kwesiphelo sangaphambili

`i18n.site` iya kupapasha umxholo wesayithi kwi `npmjs.com` ngokungagqibekanga, ngoncedo [jsdelivr.com](//jsdelivr.com) , [unpkg.com](//unpkg.com) kunye neminye imixholo `CDN` elayishwe kwi `npm` .

Ngesi siseko, imithombo yesibuko evela kwilizwe laseTshayina yongezwa ukuvumela abasebenzisi baseTshayina ukuba babe nokufikelela okuzinzileyo kwaye bafezekise **ukufumaneka okuphezulu kwesiphelo** .

Umgaqo ngulo: Yamkela izicelo ngo [`service worker`](https://developer.mozilla.org/docs/Web/API/Service_Worker_API) , zama kwakhona izicelo ezisileleyo kwezinye `CDN` , kwaye wenze ulungelelwaniso lwendawo yemvelaphi ephendula ngokukhawuleza njengomthombo wokulayisha ongagqibekanga.

[github.com/18x/serviceWorker](https://github.com/i18n-site/18x/tree/main/serviceWorker) ehambelanayo :
