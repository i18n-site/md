## <a rel=id href="#ha" id="ha"></a> Ukufumaneka okuphezulu kwesiphelo sangaphambili

`i18n.site` , umxholo wesiza uya kupapashwa ku `npmjs.com` , ngoncedo lwe [jsdelivr.com](//jsdelivr.com) [unpkg.com](//unpkg.com) kunye nomxholo omninzi `CDN` olayishwe kwi `npm` .

Ngesi siseko, imithombo yesibuko evela kwilizwe laseTshayina yongezwa ukuvumela abasebenzisi baseTshayina ukuba babe nokufikelela okuzinzileyo kwaye bafezekise **ukufumaneka okuphezulu kwesiphelo** .

Umgaqo ngulo: thintela isicelo ngoncedo [`service worker`](https://developer.mozilla.org/docs/Web/API/Service_Worker_API) , ukuba isicelo asiphumeleli, sizame kwakhona kwenye `CDN` , kwaye uvumelane nesona sikhululo siphendulayo esikhawulezayo njengomthombo wokulayisha ongagqibekanga.

Ikhowudi [github.com/18x/serviceWorker](https://github.com/i18n-site/18x/tree/main/serviceWorker) :

### Ukukhangela Okuphezulu Kufihlwe Ngokuzenzekelayo

Skrolela ezantsi kwaye ukukhangela okuphezulu kuya kuzifihla ngokuzenzekelayo.

Skrolela phezulu kwaye ukukhangela okufihliweyo kuya kuvela kwakhona.

Iza kuphelelwa xa impuku ingashukumi.

Kukho iqhosha lesikrini esigcweleyo kwikona ephezulu ngasekunene yebar yokukhangela ukwenza amava okufunda antywilayo.

Xa uskrola umxholo ngasekunene, ulwandlalo olungasekhohlo luya kuqaqambisa ngaxeshanye isahluko esifundwayo ngoku.
