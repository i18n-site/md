# Inguqulelo Yeprojekthi

Thatha iprojekthi yedemo njengomzekelo:

`en/demo2/v` yinombolo yenguqulelo yangoku yeprojekthi, eza kuboniswa ekunene kwegama leprojekthi kwibar esecaleni.

<img src="https://p.3ti.site/1721290486.avif" width="320px">

`en/` ikhowudi yolwimi ehambelana nolwimi lwemvelaphi `.i18n/conf.yml` .

Ukuba ulwimi lwakho lwentsusa ayisosiNgesi, ngoko ke `v` kufuneka ibekwe kuluhlu lweprojekthi yolwimi lwakho lomthombo. 

Ukukwazi ukukhangela iinguqulelo zembali yamaxwebhu kuphantsi kophuhliso.

Kuyacetyiswa ukuba ulungise kuphela inani loguqulelo loxwebhu xa ukhupha uhlaziyo olukhulu ( `v2` `v1`

## Sebenzisa Iifayile `v` Ukwahlulahlula Iifayile Zeeprojekthi Ezahlukeneyo

Kwiprojekthi `en/blog` , `v` `en/demo1` `en/demo2/v`

`v` ayizukuboniswa kwibar esecaleni, kodwa okoko nje i `v` ifayile ikhona, isalathiso esizimeleyo siyakwenzelwa iifayile kulawulo nakubalawuli abasezantsi.

Ngokwahlula izalathiso zeeprojekthi ezahlukeneyo, unokunqanda ukufikelela okucothayo okubangelwa kukulayisha isalathiso sazo zonke iifayile kwindawo yonke ngaxeshanye.

[https://unpkg.com/i18n.site/en/blog.json](https://unpkg.com/i18n.site/en/blog.json) , kwiprojekthi yedemo ifayile : `blog`
