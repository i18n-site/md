## Inkxaso Yoguqulo `YAML`

Isixhobo siguqulela kuphela ixabiso lesichazi-magama `YAML` , hayi izitshixo zesichazi-magama.

Ngokusekwe `YAML` , unokwakha ngokulula izisombululo zamazwe ngamazwe kwiilwimi ezahlukeneyo zokucwangcisa

## Inkxaso Yoguqulelo `HOGO`

Ixhasa uqwalaselo lweheader ye [HOGO](https://github.com/gohugoio/hugo) chwetheza ibhlog engatshintshiyo, `title` , `summary` kunye nemimandla `description` eziguqulelweyo.

## Musa Ukuguqulela Amagama Aguquguqukayo

`Markdown` njenge template ye-imeyile, `YAML` njengoqwalaselo lwefayile yolwimi, kuya kubakho iiparameters eziguquguqukayo (umzekelo: ukutshaja kwakhona `${amount}` ifunyenwe).

Olu hlobo lwegama eliguquguqukayo aliyi kuthathwa njengenguqulelo yesiNgesi `${varname}`

## Ukuphucula Uguqulo eTshayina, eJapan naseKorea

### Xa Uguqulelwa esiNgesini, Unobumba Wokuqala Wesihloko Ubhalwa Ngoonobumba Abakhulu Ngokuzenzekelayo.

ITshayina, iJapan, kunye neKorea azinabo oonobumba abakhulu okanye abancinci, kodwa izihloko zesiNgesi zidla ngoonobumba abakhulu.

`i18` inokuqonda izihloko kwi `MarkDown` , kwaye iya kwenza ngokuzenzekelayo unobumba wokuqala xa iguqulela kulwimi olunovakalelo.

`为阅读体验而优化` izakuguqulelwa njenge `Optimized for Reading Experience` .

### Amagama esiNgesi kwisiTshayina, isiJapani, isiKorea nesiTshayina Awaguqulelwanga

Amaxwebhu asuka eTshayina, eJapan naseKorea ahlala equlathe amagama amaninzi esiNgesi.

Ukuguqulelwa ngomatshini kweelwimi zesiTshayina, isiJapan kunye nesiKorea ziye zaba lulwimi olungelulo lwesiNgesi, kwaye amagama ahlala eguqulelwa kunye, njengesivakalisi saseTshayina silandelayo:

`Falcon 得分超 Llama ？Hugging Face 排名引发争议`

Ukuba usebenzisa iGoogle okanye i-DeepL, bobabini baguqulela ngokungalunganga amagama esiNgesi afanele ahlale eyintsusa (thatha isiJapan nesiFrentshi njengemizekelo).
