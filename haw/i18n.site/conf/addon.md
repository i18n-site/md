# Ho Ľopili-I

Hiki ke ho Ľonohonoho  Ľia nńĀ plug-ins ma `.i18n/conf.yml` , e like me:

```yml
addon:
  - i18n.addon/toc
```

## PńĀku Ľi KŇęhelu

* `i18n.addon/toc` :
  E hana i ka papa kuhikuhi papa kuhikuhi o `json` ma muli o `TOC` , i ho Ľohana  Ľia ma ka pa Ľamau

* `i18n.addon/mouse` :

## Hui Inoa Waihona

 ĽO nńĀ mea ho Ľopili he `npm` mau pŇę Ľolo.

 ĽO ka pŇę Ľolo e pili ana i ka `i18n.addon/toc` ma luna nei he [https://www.npmjs.com/package/@i18n.addon/toc](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@i18n.addon/toc)

Ho Ľohana ka plugin i ka mana hou loa ma ka pa Ľamau a nńĀnńĀ i nńĀ mea hou i kńďlńĀ me kńďia pule.

InńĀ makemake  Ľoe e ho Ľoponopono i ka mana, hiki ińĀ  Ľoe ke kńĀkau `i18n.addon/toc@0.1.3` .

E ho Ľokomo ka laina kauoha unuhi `i18n.site` i ka faila hui o ka pŇę Ľolo plug-in a laila e ho ĽokŇć.

 ĽO nńĀ inoa faila i  Ľaelike  Ľia penei

### htmIndex.js

`htmIndex.js` e ho Ľokomo  Ľia a hiki i ka hopena o `.i18n/htm/index.js` .

Ma kahi e pani  Ľia ai `__CONF__` me ka inoa o ka ho Ľonohonoho o kńďia manawa (e like me `dev` a i  Ľole `ol` ).

### afterTran.js

E kńĀhea  Ľia ma hope o ka pau  Ľana o ka unuhi  Ľana, a penei nńĀ  ĽńĀpana i ho Ľoholo  Ľia.

* `lang_li` : Ka papa inoa o ka  ĽŇćlelo,  Ľo ka  ĽŇćlelo mua ka  ĽŇćlelo kumu
* `changed` :
* `root` :

He puke wehewehe  ĽŇćlelo ka waiwai ho Ľiho Ľi, e like me

```json
{
  file:{
    //  path: txt, for example :
    // "_.json": "[]"
  }
}
```

 ĽO `file` ka papa inoa o nńĀ faila, `path` ke ala faila, a  Ľo `txt` ka waihona waihona.

## NńĀ Hana I KŇękulu  Ľia

Ho Ľokumu  Ľia ka `js` runtime i kŇękulu  Ľia ma ke kŇękulu lua  Ľana o [boa](https://github.com/boa-dev/boa) a  Ľo nńĀ hana i kŇękulu  Ľia e like me kńďia :

* `wPath(path, txt)` :
* `rTxt(path)` :
* `rBin(path)` :
* `rDir(dirpath)` : Heluhelu i ka papa kuhikuhi,  Ľo ka waiwai ho Ľiho Ľi he papa kuhikuhi : , papa inoa waihona
