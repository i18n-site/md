
## Ingoa Rohe Herea CNAME

Cloudflare Worker Ko te ingoa rohe ka herea ki te `SSL/TLS` → ingoa kaihautu ritenga CNAME the domain name is bound, direct access will report an error `error code: 522`

I tenei wa, me whakamahi koe i te ararere HTTP → Te ararere a nga kaimahi ki te here i tetahi ara, penei i raro nei
