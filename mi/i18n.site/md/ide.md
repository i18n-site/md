# Tohutohu Etita

Kei ia tangata tana ake etita tino pai Kia tohatohahia a maatau mahi pai ki konei `Markdown`

Ka whakamahi matou i te [vscode](https://code.visualstudio.com/) Tuhia `MarkDown` ka whakauru i te mono-in [Markdown Preview Enhanced](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=shd101wyy.markdown-preview-enhanced) hei arokite i te wa e tuhi ana.

Ka rite ki te whakaatu i raro nei, paato-matau ki te kaiwhakatika hei whakatuwhera i te matapihi arokite.

<img alt="" src="https://p.3ti.site/1720775216.avif">
<img alt="" src="https://p.3ti.site/1720775043.avif">

Whakauruhia `code xxx.md` i te raina whakahau ki te karanga `vscode` tuwhera `Markdown` konae.

Whakamahia te [PicList](https://github.com/Kuingsmile/PicList) ki te tuku pikitia, mahara ki te tautuhi i te taviri pokatata tuku, ka tuku i te whakaahua ma te paatoo.

E tautoko ana i te kape aunoa `Markdown` te whakahōputu ingoa konae i muri i te tukunga ake, e tino pai ake ana.

I te wa ano, ka taea e koe te titiro ki te whirihoranga e whai ake nei, te whakarereke i te konae, me te whirihora i te konae kua tukuna kia kopeke aunoa ki te `avif`
