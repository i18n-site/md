# Muharrir Tavsiyasi

Har bir insonning o'z sevimli muharriri bor. Keling, bu erda eng yaxshi tajribalarimizni baham ko'raylik `Markdown`

Yozish vaqtida oldindan ko'rish [Markdown Preview Enhanced](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=shd101wyy.markdown-preview-enhanced) `MarkDown` foydalanamiz [vscode](https://code.visualstudio.com/)

Quyida ko'rsatilganidek, oldindan ko'rish oynasini ochish uchun muharrirni o'ng tugmasini bosing.

<img alt="" src="https://p.3ti.site/1720775216.avif">
<img alt="" src="https://p.3ti.site/1720775043.avif">

`vscode` ochiq `Markdown` faylini chaqirish uchun buyruq satriga `code xxx.md` .

Rasmlarni yuklash uchun [PicList](https://github.com/Kuingsmile/PicList) dan foydalaning, yuklash yorliqlarini o'rnatishni unutmang va bir marta bosish bilan skrinshotni yuklang.

`Markdown` yuklangandan so'ng fayl nomini avtomatik ravishda nusxalashni qo'llab-quvvatlaydi, bu esa samaradorlikni sezilarli darajada oshiradi.

Shu bilan birga, siz quyidagi konfiguratsiyaga murojaat qilishingiz, faylni o'zgartirishingiz va yuklangan faylni rasm hajmini kamaytirish uchun avtomatik ravishda siqishni `avif` mumkin.
