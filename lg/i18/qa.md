# Ekizibu Ekya Bulijjo

## Okwongerako Oba Okusazaamu Ennyiriri Z’enkyusa, Ekivaamu Okutabulwa Mu Nkyusa

Jjukira nti **omuwendo gw’ennyiriri mu nkyusa gulina okukwatagana n’ennyiriri eziri mu kiwandiiko ekyasooka** .

Kwe kugamba, bw’oba ​​otereeza enkyusa mu ngalo, **toyongera oba tosazaamu layini za nkyusa** , bwe kitaba ekyo enkolagana ya maapu wakati w’enkyusa n’ekiwandiiko ekyasooka ejja kuba etabuddwatabuddwa.

Singa mu butanwa oyongera oba osazaamu layini, ekireeta okutabulwa, nsaba ozzaawo enkyusa mu nkyusa nga tonnaba kukyusa, `i18` odduke enkyusa , era oddemu okutereka maapu entuufu.

Maapu wakati w'enkyusa n'ekiwandiiko eky'olubereberye esibiddwa ku kabonero `.i18h/hash` akabonero akapya mu [i18n.site/token](//i18n.site/token)

## `YAML` : Y'okuziyiza `HTML` Ya Link Okukyusibwa Okudda Mu `Markdown`

Omuwendo gwa `YAML` gujja kutwalibwa nga `MarkDown` okuvvuunula.

Oluusi okukyusa kwa `HTML` → `MarkDown` si kye twagala, gamba nga `<a href="/">Home</a>` okukyusibwa okudda mu `[Home](/)` .

Yongera ekintu kyonna okuggyako `href` `<a class="A" href="/">Home</a>` `a`

## `./I18n/Hash` Fayiro Zino Wammanga Zikontana

Ssaako fayiro ezikontana era oddemu okuddukanya `i18`
