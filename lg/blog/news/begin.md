# i18n.site · Ekikozesebwa Mu Kuvvuunula N'okuzimba Omukutu Gwa MarkDown Kati Kiri Ku Mutimbagano!

Oluvannyuma lw'ekitundu ky'omwaka ogusoba mu nkulaakulana, [https://i18n.site](//i18n.site)

Mu kiseera kino, ebikozesebwa bibiri eby’ennyiriri z’ebiragiro eby’ensibuko enzigule biteekebwa mu nkola:

* `i18` : MarkDown Ekikozesebwa mu kuvvuunula layini y'ebiragiro
* `i18n.site` : Omukutu gw'ebiwandiiko ebitali bikyuka ogw'ennimi nnyingi, **erongooseddwa okusobola okusoma**

Okuvvuunula kusobola okukuuma obulungi enkola ya `Markdown` . Asobola okuzuula enkyukakyuka mu fayiro n'okuvvuunula fayiro zokka ezirina enkyukakyuka.

Enkyusa esobola okulongoosebwamu n’oddamu okugivvuunula mu kyuma, enkyukakyuka mu ngalo mu nkyusa tezijja kuwandiikibwako (singa akatundu kano ak’ekiwandiiko ekyasooka tekakyusiddwa).

[➤ Nyiga wano okukkiriza n'okugoberera github i18n.site](https://github.com/login/oauth/authorize?client_id=Ov23liuGAmK0plc9FgB3&amp;scope=user:email,user:follow,public_repo) n'ofuna **bonus $50** .

## Ensibuko

Mu mulembe gwa yintaneeti, ensi yonna katale, era okuyiga ennimi nnyingi n’okubeera mu kitundu bye bukugu obusookerwako.

Ebikozesebwa mu kuddukanya okuvvuunula ebiriwo bizito nnyo Eri abakola pulogulaamu abeesigama ku nzirukanya y’enkyusa `git` , bakyasinga kwagala layini y’ekiragiro.

Kale, nakola ekintu eky’okuvvuunula `i18` era ne nzimba ekintu ekiyitibwa multi-language static site generator `i18n.site` nga nsinziira ku kikozesebwa mu kuvvuunula.

![](https://p.3ti.site/1723777556.avif)

Eno ntandikwa yokka, waliwo bingi eby’okukola.

Okugeza, nga oyunga omukutu gw’ebiwandiiko ebitali bikyuka ku mikutu gya yintaneeti n’okuwandiika ku email, abakozesa basobola okutuukirirwa mu budde ng’ebipya bifulumiziddwa.

Okugeza, enkiiko z’ennimi nnyingi n’enkola z’okulagira emirimu bisobola okuteekebwa mu mukutu gwonna, okusobozesa abakozesa okuwuliziganya awatali biziyiza.

## Ensonda Enzigule

Layini y'ekiragiro [js Engine boa_engine](https://docs.rs/boa_engine) , [eyingiziddwa database fjall](https://github.com/fjall-rs/fjall) .
