# Common Problem

## Kuwedzera Kana Kudzima Mitsetse Yeshanduro, Zvichikonzera Kuvhiringika Mushanduro

Yeuka, **nhamba yemitsara mushanduro inofanira kuenderana nemitsara iri mumagwaro epakutanga** .

Kureva kuti, kana uchigadzirisa shanduro nemaoko, **usawedzera kana kudzima mitsetse yeshanduro** , zvikasadaro hukama hwemepu pakati peshandurudzo nemavara ekutanga huchakanganisika.

Kana ukawedzera kana kudzima mutsara netsaona, zvichikonzera nyonganiso, ndapota dzosa shanduro kushanduro isati yagadziridzwa, mhanyisa shanduro `i18` , uye chengetedza zvakare mepu chaiyo.

Mamepu pakati peshanduro nemavara ekutanga anosungirirwa kuchiratidzo Gadzira chiratidzo chitsva mu [i18n.site/token](//i18n.site/token) bvisa `.i18h/hash` uye shandura zvakare kudzima mepu inovhiringika (asi izvi zvichaita kuti zvese zvigadziriso zvemanyorero zvirasikike).

## `YAML` : Ekudzivirira `HTML` Yeiyi Link Kuti Isashandurwe Kuita `Markdown`

`MarkDown` kwe `YAML`

Dzimwe nguva shanduko `HTML` → `MarkDown` haisiriyo yatinoda, senge `<a href="/">Home</a>` kushandurwa kuita `[Home](/)` .

Wedzera chero hunhu kunze kwe `href` kune `a` tag, senge `<a class="A" href="/">Home</a>` , kudzivirira kutendeuka uku.
