# Kurudziro Yemupepeti

Munhu wese ane mupepeti wake waanofarira `Markdown`

Isu tinoshandisa `MarkDown` [vscode](https://code.visualstudio.com/) isa iyo plug-in [Markdown Preview Enhanced](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=shd101wyy.markdown-preview-enhanced) kuti utarise munguva chaiyo uchinyora.

Sezvinoratidzwa pazasi, tinya-kurudyi mupepeti kuti uvhure hwindo rekuona.

<img alt="" src="https://p.3ti.site/1720775043.avif">

`code xxx.md` pamutsara wekuraira kufonera `vscode` vhura `Markdown` faira.

Shandisa [PicList](https://github.com/Kuingsmile/PicList) kurodha mapikicha, rangarira kuseta kiyi yekudimbudzira, uye kurodha iyo skrini nekudzvanya kumwe chete.

Inotsigira otomatiki kukopa iyo faira `Markdown` mushure mekuisa, iyo inovandudza zvakanyanya kushanda zvakanaka.

Panguva imwecheteyo, iwe unogona kutarisa kune inotevera gadziriso, shandura iyo faira, uye gadzirisa iyo yakarodha faira kuti `avif` otomatiki kudzikisa saizi yemufananidzo.
