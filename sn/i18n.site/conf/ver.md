## Shandisa Isina `v` Mafaera Kupatsanura Mafaera Mapurojekiti Akasiyana

Muchirongwa chedemo, mukuwedzera `en/demo2/v` , unogona zvakare kuona kuti zviri mukati me `en/blog` uye `en/demo1` madhairekitori haana chinhu `v` mafaera.

Empty `v` haizoonekwe mudivi repepa, asi chero bedzi iyo `v` faira iripo, yakazvimirira index ichagadzirwa yemafaira ari mudhairekitori uye subdirectories.

Nekuparadzanisa ma index emapurojekiti akasiyana, unogona kudzivirira kupinda zvishoma nezvishoma kunokonzerwa nekurodha index yemafaira ese mune yese saiti kamwechete.

Semuenzaniso, muchirongwa chedemo : [https://unpkg.com/i18n.site/en/blog.json](https://unpkg.com/i18n.site/en/blog.json) `blog`
