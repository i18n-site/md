## <a rel=id href="#ha" id="ha"></a> Kumberi-kumagumo kuwanikwa kwepamusoro

`i18n.site` , zvirimo zvesaiti zvichaburitswa ku `npmjs.com` , nerubatsiro rwe [jsdelivr.com](//jsdelivr.com) [unpkg.com](//unpkg.com) uye akawanda `CDN` zvirimo zvakaiswa pa `npm` .

Pahwaro uhwu, masosi egirazi kubva kumahombekombe eChina akawedzerwa kuti abvumire vashandisi veChinese kuwana yakagadzikana uye kuwana **yakakwirira-yekupedzisira-kuwanikwa** .

Nheyo yacho ndeiyi: tambira chikumbiro nerubatsiro [`service worker`](https://developer.mozilla.org/docs/Web/API/Service_Worker_API) , kana chikumbiro chikatadza, edzazve pane imwe `CDN` , uye neadaptive inogonesa kukurumidza kupindura kwekutanga chiteshi seyakagadzika yekurodha sosi.

Related [github.com/18x/serviceWorker](https://github.com/i18n-site/18x/tree/main/serviceWorker) :
