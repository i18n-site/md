# Matsalar Gama Gari

## Ƙara Ko Share Layin Fassarar, Yana Haifar Da Rudani a Cikin Fassarar

Ka tuna, **adadin layukan da ke cikin fassarar dole ne su dace da layin da ke cikin ainihin rubutun** .

Wato, lokacin da ake daidaita fassarar da hannu, **kar a ƙara ko share layin fassarar** , in ba haka ba dangantakar taswira tsakanin fassarar da ainihin rubutun za ta lalace.

Idan ka ƙara ko share layi da gangan, yana haifar da rudani, da fatan za a mayar da fassarar zuwa sigar kafin gyara, `i18` gudanar da fassarar, kuma sake cache taswirar daidai.

Taswirar taswira tsakanin fassarar da ainihin rubutun yana daure da alamar a [i18n.site/token](//i18n.site/token) share `.i18h/hash` kuma a sake fassarawa don share taswirar ruɗani (amma wannan zai haifar da asarar duk gyare-gyaren hannu ga fassarar).

## `YAML` : A Hana `HTML` Na Hanyar Haɗin Gwiwar Canzawa Zuwa `Markdown`

Za a kula da ƙimar `YAML` a matsayin `MarkDown` don fassarar.

Wani lokaci `HTML` → `MarkDown` ba shine abin da muke so ba, kamar ana canza `<a href="/">Home</a>` zuwa `[Home](/)` .

Ƙara kowane sifa banda `href` zuwa `a` , kamar `<a class="A" href="/">Home</a>` , don guje wa wannan jujjuya.
