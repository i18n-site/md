
## CNAME Daure Sunan Yankin

An ɗaure sunan yankin ta hanyar `SSL/TLS` → sunan mai masaukin al'ada CNAME an ɗaure sunan yankin, samun damar kai tsaye zai `error code: 522` da rahoton kuskure Cloudflare Worker

A wannan lokacin, kuna buƙatar amfani da hanyar hanyar HTTP → Ma'aikata don ɗaure hanya, kamar yadda aka nuna a ƙasa.
