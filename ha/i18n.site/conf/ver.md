# Sigar Aikin

Dauki aikin demo a matsayin misali:

`en/demo2/v` shine lambar sigar aikin na yanzu, wanda za'a nuna shi a hannun dama na sunan aikin a cikin shaci.

<img src="https://p.3ti.site/1721290486.avif" width="320px">

`en/` shine lambar yare mai dacewa da harshen tushen fassarar `.i18n/conf.yml` da aka saita.

Idan tushen harshen ku ba Ingilishi ba ne, to ya kamata a sanya fayil ɗin `v` 

Ƙarfin bincika nau'ikan takardu na tarihi yana ƙarƙashin haɓakawa.

Ana ba da shawarar canza lambar sigar daftarin aiki kawai lokacin fitar da manyan sabuntawa (kamar `v1` , `v2` ) don guje wa ƙunshewar shafukan da injunan bincike suka yi maƙasudin saboda yawan sigar lambobi.

## Yi Amfani Da `v` Fayiloli Don Raba Fihirisar Fayil Don Ayyuka Daban-Daban

A cikin aikin demo, ban da `en/demo2/v` kuna iya ganin cewa abubuwan da ke cikin kundayen adireshi `en/blog` da `en/demo1` fanko ne `v` fayil.

Ba za a nuna komai `v` a cikin jigon labarun gefe, amma idan dai `v` fayil ɗin ya wanzu, za a samar da fihirisar mai zaman kanta don fayilolin da ke cikin kundin adireshi da ƙananan bayanai.

Ta hanyar rarraba firikwensin ayyuka daban-daban, zaku iya guje wa jinkirin samun dama ta hanyar loda fihirisar duk fayiloli a cikin rukunin yanar gizon gaba ɗaya.
