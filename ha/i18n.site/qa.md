# Matsalar Gama Gari

## An Goge Kwatsam `.i18n/V` Yana Haifar Da Gaza Buga Kunshin `Npm`

An adana sigar tarihi ta sakin `npm` a cikin `.i18n/v/ol/v.hash`

Idan kun share `.i18n/v/ol` ba za a buga kunshin `npm` ba.

A wannan lokacin, fara nemo lambar sigar sigar sakin aikin ku a cikin [npmjs.com](//npmjs.com) , misali `0.1.9`

Sannan koma zuwa `bash`

```bash
mkdir -p .i18n/v/ol
echo @0.1.9 > .i18n/v/ol/v.hash
```

Lura cewa gyara ta wannan hanyar zai rasa tarihin fayil ɗin, yana sa ba zai yiwu a ƙara sabunta sakin gaba ba, kuma duk abun ciki za a sake tattarawa kuma a loda shi sau ɗaya.
