# Tsarin Tsarin MarkDown

## Makiyin Anga

Na `MarkDown` an samar da su ne bisa abubuwan da ke cikin rubutu A cikin yanayi na yare da yawa, wannan maganin ba zai yiwu ba.

Maganin anga `i18n.site` aka yarda shine a saka rubutu mai kama da `<a rel=id href="#xxx" id="xxx"></a>` in `MarkDown` don ƙirƙirar anka mai sanyawa da hannu.

`<a rel=id href="#xxx" id="xxx"></a>` anan `rel=id` yana bayyana salon shafi na ma'anar anga, da fatan za a maye gurbin `xxx` da ainihin gajartar ku ta Ingilishi.

Yawancin lokaci ana ƙara anchors zuwa taken, kamar:

```md
### <a rel=id href="#i18" id="i18"></a>i18 : MarkDown Command Line Translation Tool
```

## Rubuta `HTML` a `MarkDown`

Za a iya `HTML` `pug` cikin lambar.

Ba za a fassara abun ciki a cikin kashi `<pre>`

Haɗa waɗannan maki biyu, zaku iya rubuta `HTML` cikin `MarkDown` sauƙi don cimma tasirin nuni iri-iri.

Kuna iya komawa ga aiwatarwa a cikin [github.com/i18n-site/md/en/README.md](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/i18n-site/md/main/en/README.md) lambar kamar haka :

```html
<h1 style="display:flex;justify-content:space-between">i18n.site ⋅ International Solutions<img src="//p.3ti.site/logo.svg" style="user-select:none;margin-top:-1px;width:42px"></h1>
<pre class="langli" style="display:flex;flex-wrap:wrap;background:transparent;border:1px solid #eee;font-size:12px;box-shadow:0 0 3px inset #eee;padding:12px 5px 4px 12px;justify-content:space-between;"><style>pre.langli i{font-weight:300;font-family:s;margin-right:2px;margin-bottom:8px;font-style:normal;color:#666;border-bottom:1px dashed #ccc;}</style><i>English</i><i>简体中文</i><i>Deutsch</i> … …</pre>
```

Lura cewa `<style>` kuma an bayyana shi `<pre>` sama.
