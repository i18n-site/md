# Likanisi Ya Mobongisi-Nzela

Moto na moto azali na mobongisi na ye oyo alingaka mingi Tokabola mayele na biso ya malamu awa `Markdown`

Tosalelaka [vscode](https://code.visualstudio.com/) [Markdown Preview Enhanced](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=shd101wyy.markdown-preview-enhanced) `MarkDown`

Ndenge emonisami awa na nse, finá na lobɔkɔ ya mobali na mobongisi mpo na kofungola lininisa ya aperçu.

Tyá `code xxx.md` na molɔngɔ ya komande mpo na kobenga fisyé `vscode` kofungola `Markdown` .

Salelá [PicList](https://github.com/Kuingsmile/PicList)

Ezali ko soutenir automatiquement ko copier format ya kombo ya fichier `Markdown`
