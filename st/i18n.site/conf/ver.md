## Sebelisa Lifaele Tse Se Nang `v` Ho Arola Li-Index Bakeng Sa Merero E Fapaneng

Morerong oa demo, ntle le `en/demo2/v` o ka bona hape hore litaba tsa li-directory tsa `en/blog` le `en/demo1` ha li na letho `v` lifaele.

Ha e na letho `v` e ke ke ea hlahisoa lethathamong le ka thoko, empa ha feela faele ea `v` e ntse e le teng, index e ikemetseng e tla hlahisoa bakeng sa lifaele tse bukeng le li-subdirectories.

Ka ho arola li-index tsa merero e fapaneng, u ka qoba phihlello e liehang e bakoang ke ho kenya index ea lifaele tsohle setšeng kaofela hang-hang.

Ka mohlala, morerong [https://unpkg.com/i18n.site/en/blog.json](https://unpkg.com/i18n.site/en/blog.json) : `blog`
