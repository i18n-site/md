# Khothaletso Ea Mohlophisi

E mong le e mong o na le mohlophisi oa hae eo a mo ratang `Markdown`

Re sebelisa [vscode](https://code.visualstudio.com/) `MarkDown` me u kenye plug-in [Markdown Preview Enhanced](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=shd101wyy.markdown-preview-enhanced) ho bona esale pele ka nako ea nnete ha u ntse u ngola.

Joalokaha ho bontšitsoe ka tlase, tobetsa ka ho le letona ho mohlophisi ho bula fensetere ea ponelopele.

<img alt="" src="https://p.3ti.site/1720775216.avif">
<img alt="" src="https://p.3ti.site/1720775043.avif">

`code xxx.md` moleng oa taelo ho letsetsa `vscode` bula `Markdown` file.

Sebelisa [PicList](https://github.com/Kuingsmile/PicList) ho kenya linepe, hopola ho seta konopo ea ho kenya, 'me u kenye skrini ka ho tobetsa hanngoe feela.

E ts'ehetsa ho kopitsa lebitso la faele ka bo `Markdown` ka mor'a ho kenya, e leng ho ntlafatsang ts'ebetso e ntle haholo.

Ka nako e ts'oanang, o ka bua ka tlhophiso e latelang, fetola faele, 'me u hlophise faele e kentsoeng hore e hatellehe ka bo `avif` ho fokotsa boholo ba setšoantšo.
