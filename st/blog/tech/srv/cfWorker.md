
## CNAME E Tlamahaneng Le Lebitso La Domain

Cloudflare Worker 's domain name e tlameletsoe ka `SSL/TLS` → lebitso la moamoheli CNAME hore lebitso la domain le tlameletsoe, phihlello e tobileng e tla tlaleha phoso `error code: 522`

Ka nako ena, o hloka ho sebelisa HTTP routing → Mokhoa oa basebetsi ho tlama tsela, joalo ka ha ho bonts'itsoe ka tlase.
