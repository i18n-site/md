# Ikibazo Rusange

## Ku Bw'impanuka Wasibwe, Bigatuma `Npm` Pack Idashobora Gutangazwa `.i18n/V`

`.i18n/v/ol/v.hash` Amateka ya verisiyo yo `npm` yabitswe muri!

Niba usibye kubwimpanuka `.i18n/v/ol` `npm` Pack ntabwo izatangazwa.

Muri iki gihe, banza ushakishe verisiyo yimibare yanyuma yo gusohora umushinga wawe [npmjs.com](//npmjs.com) kurugero `0.1.9`

Noneho reba kuri Kurema dosiye hepfo `bash`

```bash
mkdir -p .i18n/v/ol
echo @0.1.9 > .i18n/v/ol/v.hash
```

Menya ko gusana murubu buryo bizatakaza amateka ya dosiye, bigatuma bidashoboka kuvugurura buhoro buhoro ibyasohotse ubutaha, nibirimo byose bizasubirwamo kandi bishyirwe rimwe.
