## <a rel=id href="#ha" id="ha"></a> Imbere-iherezo irahari

`i18n.site` , `CDN` kurubuga bizashyirwa [unpkg.com](//unpkg.com) kuri `npmjs.com` , `npm` [jsdelivr.com](//jsdelivr.com)

Hashingiwe kuri ibyo, indorerwamo zituruka ku mugabane w’Ubushinwa zongerewe kugira ngo abakoresha Ubushinwa babone uburyo buhamye kandi bagere **ku ntera yo hejuru iboneka** .

Ihame ni: guhagarika icyifuzo ubifashijwemo na [`service worker`](https://developer.mozilla.org/docs/Web/API/Service_Worker_API) niba icyifuzo cyananiranye, ongera ugerageze kurindi `CDN` , kandi uhindure uburyo bwihuse bwihuse bwihuse nkibisanzwe bitwara ibintu.
