# Imiterere Yumushinga

Fata umushinga wa demo nk'urugero:

`en/demo2/v`

<img src="https://p.3ti.site/1721290486.avif" width="320px">

`.i18n/conf.yml` `en/`

Niba ururimi rwawe ruturuka atari Icyongereza, noneho `v` igomba gushyirwa mububiko bwumushinga wururimi rwawe. 

Ubushobozi bwo gushakisha amateka yinyandiko zirimo gutezwa imbere.

Birasabwa guhindura gusa nimero ya verisiyo yinyandiko mugihe urekura amakuru mashya (nka `v1` , `v2` ) Kugirango wirinde guhuzagurika impapuro zerekanwe na moteri zishakisha kubera nimero nyinshi cyane.

## Koresha `v` Kugirango Ugabanye Indangagaciro Za Dosiye Kumishinga Itandukanye

Mu mushinga wa demo, usibye `en/demo2/v` , urashobora kandi kubona ko ibiri mububiko bwa `en/blog` na `en/demo1` ari ubusa `v` dosiye.

`v` ntabwo bizerekanwa kumurongo wuruhande, ariko mugihe cyose `v` dosiye ibaho, indangagaciro yigenga izabyara amadosiye mububiko no mubuyobozi.

Mugabanye indangagaciro yimishinga itandukanye, urashobora kwirinda kwinjira buhoro biterwa no gupakira indangagaciro ya dosiye zose kurubuga rwose icyarimwe.

Kurugero, mumushinga wa demo dosiye [https://unpkg.com/i18n.site/en/blog.json](https://unpkg.com/i18n.site/en/blog.json) : `blog`
