# Icyifuzo Cya Muhinduzi

Umuntu wese afite umwanditsi akunda Reka dusangire imyitozo myiza hano `Markdown`

Twifashishije [vscode](https://code.visualstudio.com/) `MarkDown` ushyireho plug-in [Markdown Preview Enhanced](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=shd101wyy.markdown-preview-enhanced) kugirango urebe mbere mugihe wandika!

Nkuko bigaragara hepfo, kanda iburyo-mwanditsi kugirango ufungure idirishya.

`code xxx.md` kumurongo wumurongo wo guhamagara `vscode` fungura `Markdown` dosiye.

[PicList](https://github.com/Kuingsmile/PicList) kugirango ushireho amashusho, ibuka gushiraho urufunguzo rwo kohereza, hanyuma wohereze amashusho ukanze.

Ifasha mu buryo bwikora kwigana imiterere ya dosiye nyuma yo kohereza, itezimbere cyane imikorere `Markdown`
