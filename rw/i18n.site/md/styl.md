
### Reba `> [!NOTE]`

> [!NOTE]
> Niba unyoherereje ubutumwa ngasubiza ako kanya, bivuze iki?
> Ibi birerekana ko nkunda gukina na terefone zigendanwa.


### Kuburira `> [!WARN]`

> [!WARN]
> Iyo ugiye mubikorwa byo mwishyamba, ni ngombwa kurinda umutekano.
> Mu cyumweru gishize, itsinda ry’abazamuka bahuye n’umuyaga hagati y’umusozi biba ngombwa ko bahunga kubera ko bananiwe kugenzura iteganyagihe.
> Wibuke, ibikorwa byose byo hanze bisaba kwitegura bihagije no kwitondera ikirere.
