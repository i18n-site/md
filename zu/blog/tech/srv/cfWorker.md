
## Igama Lesizinda Eliboshwe Nge-CNAME

Igama lesizinda liboshelwe ku `SSL/TLS` → igama lesizinda sangokwezifiso CNAME Cloudflare Worker igama lesizinda liboshiwe, ukufinyelela okuqondile kuzobika iphutha `error code: 522`

Ngalesi sikhathi, udinga ukusebenzisa umzila we-HTTP → Umzila wabasebenzi ukuze ubophe umzila, njengoba kukhonjisiwe ngezansi.
