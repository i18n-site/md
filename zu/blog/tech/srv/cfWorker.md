
## Igama Lesizinda Eliboshwe Nge-CNAME

Cloudflare Worker Igama lesizinda liphasisiwe `SSL/TLS` Igama lomsingathi elenziwe ngokwezifiso liyabopha CNAME Ngemuva kwegama lesizinda, ukufinyelela okuqondile kuzobika iphutha `error code: 522`

Ngalesi sikhathi, udinga ukusebenzisa umzila we-HTTP → Umzila wabasebenzi ukuze ubophe umzila, njengoba kukhonjisiwe ngezansi.
