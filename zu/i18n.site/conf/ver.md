# Inguqulo Yephrojekthi

Thatha iphrojekthi yedemo njengesibonelo:

`en/demo2/v` inombolo yenguqulo yamanje yephrojekthi, ezoboniswa kwesokudla segama lephrojekthi kubha eseceleni.

<img src="https://p.3ti.site/1721290486.avif" width="320px">

`en/` ikhodi yolimi ehambisana nolimi oluwumthombo wokuhumusha olumisiwe ongu- `.i18n/conf.yml` .

Uma ulimi lwakho lomthombo kungesona isiNgisi, ifayela le- `v` 

Ikhono lokuphequlula izinguqulo zomlando zamadokhumenti lisathuthukiswa.

Kunconywa ukuthi uguqule inombolo yenguqulo yedokhumenti kuphela lapho ukhipha izibuyekezo ezinkulu (ezifana `v1` , `v2` ) ukuze ugweme ukuhlanganisa amakhasi akhonjwe izinjini zokusesha ngenxa yezinombolo zenguqulo eziningi kakhulu.

## Sebenzisa Amafayela `v` Ukuze Uhlukanise Izinkomba Zamafayela Kumaphrojekthi Ahlukene

Kuphrojekthi yedemo, ngaphezu `en/demo2/v` , ungabona futhi ukuthi okuqukethwe kwezikhombisi ze- `en/blog` kanye ne- `en/demo1` akunalutho `v` amafayela.

Akunalutho `v` ngeke kuboniswe kubha eseceleni kohlaka, kodwa inqobo nje uma ifayela elingu- `v` lisekhona, kuzokwenziwa inkomba ezimele yamafayela kuhla lwemibhalo nasezindaweni ezingaphansi.

Ngokuhlukanisa izinkomba zamaphrojekthi ahlukene, ungagwema ukufinyelela okunensayo okubangelwa ukulayisha inkomba yawo wonke amafayela kusayithi lonke ngesikhathi esisodwa.
