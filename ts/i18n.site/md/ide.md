# Xitsundzuxo Xa Muhleri

Un’wana na un’wana u na muhleri ​​wa yena loyi a n’wi tsakelaka A hi avelaneni maendlelo ya hina lamanene laha `Markdown`

Hi tirhisa [vscode](https://code.visualstudio.com/) Tsala `MarkDown` na ku nghenisa plug-in [Markdown Preview Enhanced](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=shd101wyy.markdown-preview-enhanced) ku languta ka ha ri emahlweni hi nkarhi wa xiviri loko hi ri karhi hi tsala.

Hilaha swi kombisiweke hakona laha hansi, right-click eka editor ku pfula fasitere ra preview.

Nghenisa `code xxx.md` eka layini ya swileriso ku vitana `vscode` open `Markdown` file.

Tirhisa [PicList](https://github.com/Kuingsmile/PicList) ku layicha swifaniso, tsundzuka ku veka xilotlelo xa ndlela yo koma yo layicha, ivi u layicha xifaniso xa xikirini hi ku tikhoma kan’we.

Yi seketela ku kopa hi ku tisungulela xivumbeko xa vito ra `Markdown` endzhaku ko layicha, leswi antswisaka swinene vukorhokeri.

Hi nkarhi lowu fanaka, u nga languta eka xivumbeko lexi landzelaka, u cinca fayili, ivi u lulamisa fayili leyi layichiweke leswaku yi tshikileriwa hi ku tisungulela eka `avif`
