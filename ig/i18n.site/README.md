## Okwu Mmalite

`i18n.site` bụ echiche m banyere ụdị mmepe webụsaịtị.

Nke a bụ ihe na-emepụta saịtị akwụkwọ na usoro mmepe webụsaịtị.

Ọhụụ ọhụrụ nke mmepe webụsaịtị gbadoro ụkwụ na `MarkDown`

Akụkụ ihu njedebe ọ bụla bụ ngwugwu enwere ike itinye ya n'onwe ya.

Na ndabere nke nkewa nke ihu-n'ihu na azụ azụ, e nwekwara nkewa nke ọdịnaya static na ike data.

Ewubere ihe ị na [i18n.site](/) eleta dabere na usoro a (gụnyere sistemu onye ọrụ, sistemụ ịgba ụgwọ, ndenye aha email, wdg).
