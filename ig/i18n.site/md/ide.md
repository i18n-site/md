# Nkwanye Onye Ndezi

Onye ọ bụla nwere onye nchịkọta akụkọ kachasị amasị ya Ka anyị kesaa omume anyị kacha mma ebe a `Markdown`

Anyị na [vscode](https://code.visualstudio.com/) [Markdown Preview Enhanced](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=shd101wyy.markdown-preview-enhanced) dee `MarkDown`

Dị ka egosiri n'okpuru, pịa aka nri na nchịkọta akụkọ iji mepee windo nlele.
