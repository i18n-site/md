# Nsogbu Nkịtị

## Na Mberede Ehichapụrụ `.i18n/V` Na-Eme Ka Ebipụ `Npm` Ngwugwu

A na-echekwa ụdị mbipụta `npm` nke akụkọ ihe mere eme na `.i18n/v/ol/v.hash`

Ọ bụrụ na ịhichapụ na mberede `.i18n/v/ol` agaghị ebipụta ngwugwu `npm` ahụ.

N'oge a, buru ụzọ chọta nọmba ụdị nke mbipute ikpeazụ nke ọrụ gị `0.1.9` [npmjs.com](//npmjs.com)

Mgbe ahụ rụtụ aka `bash` Mepụta faịlụ n'okpuru.

```bash
mkdir -p .i18n/v/ol
echo @0.1.9 > .i18n/v/ol/v.hash
```

Rịba ama na ịrụzi n'ụzọ dị otú a ga-atụfu akụkọ ihe mere eme nke faịlụ ahụ, na-eme ka ọ ghara ikwe omume ịbawanye ntọhapụ na-esote, na ọdịnaya niile ga-ebughachi ma bulite otu ugboro.
