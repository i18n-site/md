## <a rel=id href="#ha" id="ha"></a> Pem hauv ntej-kawg siab muaj

`i18n.site` Los ntawm lub neej ntawd, cov ntsiab lus ntawm lub xaib yuav raug tshaj tawm rau `npmjs.com` , nrog kev pab ntawm [jsdelivr.com](//jsdelivr.com) [unpkg.com](//unpkg.com) thiab ntau yam `CDN` cov ntsiab lus loaded ntawm `npm` .

Raws li lub hauv paus no, daim iav qhov chaw los ntawm Tuam Tshoj mainland tau ntxiv los tso cai rau cov neeg siv Suav kom muaj kev ruaj ntseg thiab ua tiav **cov khoom muaj nyob rau pem hauv ntej** .

`CDN` hauv paus ntsiab lus yog: cuam tshuam qhov kev thov nrog kev pab ntawm [`service worker`](https://developer.mozilla.org/docs/Web/API/Service_Worker_API)
