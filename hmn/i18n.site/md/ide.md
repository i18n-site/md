# Editor Pom Zoo

Txhua tus muaj lawv tus kheej nyiam editor Cia qhia peb cov kev coj ua zoo tshaj plaws ntawm no `Markdown`

Peb siv [vscode](https://code.visualstudio.com/) [Markdown Preview Enhanced](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=shd101wyy.markdown-preview-enhanced) `MarkDown`

Raws li qhia hauv qab no, right-click nyob rau hauv lub editor qhib lub qhov rais saib ua ntej.

Nkag mus `code xxx.md` ntawm kab hais kom hu `vscode` qhib `Markdown` cov ntaub ntawv.

Siv [PicList](https://github.com/Kuingsmile/PicList) txhawm rau upload duab, nco ntsoov teeb tsa tus yuam sij upload shortcut, thiab upload lub screenshot nrog nias ib zaug.
