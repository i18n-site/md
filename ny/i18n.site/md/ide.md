# Malingaliro a Mkonzi

Aliyense ali ndi mkonzi wake yemwe amakonda `Markdown`

Timagwiritsa [Markdown Preview Enhanced](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=shd101wyy.markdown-preview-enhanced) [vscode](https://code.visualstudio.com/) Lembani `MarkDown`

Monga m'munsimu, dinani kumanja mu mkonzi kutsegula chithunzithunzi zenera.
