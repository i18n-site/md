# Mtundu Wa Polojekiti

Tengani projekiti yowonetsera monga chitsanzo:

`en/demo2/v` ndi nambala yamakono ya pulojekitiyi, yomwe idzasonyezedwe kumanja kwa dzina la polojekiti mu autilaini yam'mbali.

<img src="https://p.3ti.site/1721290486.avif" width="320px">

`en/` khodi ya chinenero chofanana ndi `.i18n/conf.yml` translation chinenero.

Ngati chinenero chanu si Chingerezi, ndiye kuti fayilo ya `v` 

Kutha kusakatula zolembedwa zakale kukukonzedwa.

Ndikofunikira kuti mungosintha nambala yachikalatacho potulutsa zosintha zazikulu (monga `v1` , `v2` ) kuti mupewe kusokoneza masamba omwe ali ndi injini zosakira chifukwa cha manambala ochulukirapo.

## Gwiritsani Ntchito Mafayilo Opanda `v` Kuti Mugawane Zolemba Zama Projekiti Osiyanasiyana

Mu polojekitiyi, kuwonjezera `en/demo2/v` , mutha kuwonanso kuti zomwe zili m'mawu `en/blog` ndi `en/demo1` zilibe kanthu `v` mafayilo.

Zopanda `v` `v`
