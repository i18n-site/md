# Iṣeduro Olootu

Gbogbo eniyan ni olootu ayanfẹ ti ara wọn Jẹ ki a pin awọn iṣe wa ti o dara julọ nibi `Markdown`

A `MarkDown` [Markdown Preview Enhanced](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=shd101wyy.markdown-preview-enhanced) [vscode](https://code.visualstudio.com/)

Bi a ṣe han ni isalẹ, tẹ-ọtun ni olootu lati ṣii window awotẹlẹ.

<img alt="" src="https://p.3ti.site/1720775216.avif">
<img alt="" src="https://p.3ti.site/1720775043.avif">

Tẹ `code xxx.md` lori laini aṣẹ lati pe `vscode` faili `Markdown` ṣii.

Lo [PicList](https://github.com/Kuingsmile/PicList) lati gbe awọn aworan silẹ, ranti lati ṣeto bọtini ọna abuja ikojọpọ, ati gbe sikirinifoto pẹlu titẹ kan.

O ṣe atilẹyin didakọ orukọ `Markdown` laifọwọyi lẹhin ikojọpọ, eyiti o mu ilọsiwaju dara si.

Ni akoko kanna, o le tọka si iṣeto ni atẹle, yi faili naa pada, ki o tunto faili ti o gbejade lati fisinuirindigbindigbin laifọwọyi si `avif`
