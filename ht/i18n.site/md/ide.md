# Rekòmandasyon Editè

Tout moun gen pwòp editè pi renmen an. Ann pataje pi bon pratik nou yo isit la `Markdown`

Nou itilize Write [vscode](https://code.visualstudio.com/) epi enstale plug-in [Markdown Preview Enhanced](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=shd101wyy.markdown-preview-enhanced) pou preview an tan reyèl pandan w `MarkDown` ekri.

Jan yo montre anba a, klike sou editè a pou w ouvri fenèt preview la.

<img alt="" src="https://p.3ti.site/1720775216.avif">
<img alt="" src="https://p.3ti.site/1720775043.avif">

`code xxx.md` nan liy lòd la pou rele `vscode` louvri `Markdown` fichye.

Sèvi ak [PicList](https://github.com/Kuingsmile/PicList) pou telechaje foto, sonje mete kle rakoursi telechaje a, epi telechaje ekran an ak yon sèl klike sou.

Li sipòte otomatikman kopye fòma non fichye `Markdown` apre w fin telechaje, sa ki amelyore anpil efikasite.

An menm tan an, ou ka refere a konfigirasyon sa a, modifye fichye a, epi konfigirasyon fichye a telechaje yo dwe otomatikman konprese nan `avif`
